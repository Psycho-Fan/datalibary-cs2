CS2 Maps

Dust II

Dust II is one of the most iconic maps in Counter-Strike history, known for its balanced layout and intense mid control. The map features two bombsites and is set in a Middle Eastern town.

Mirage

Mirage is a popular competitive map set in a North African city. It offers a variety of strategic options for both teams, with open mid areas and well-defined bombsites.

Inferno

Inferno is famous for its narrow chokepoints and tactical gameplay. The map is set in an Italian village and requires strong team coordination, especially around the banana and apartments areas.

Nuke

Nuke is a unique map with a vertical layout, featuring both upper and lower bombsites. Set in a nuclear power plant, it challenges players with its complex rotations and multiple entry points.

Ancient

Ancient is a newer addition to the CS2 map pool, set in a mysterious jungle temple. It features tight corridors and open spaces, rewarding both aggressive and methodical playstyles.

Overpass

Overpass is a map set in Berlin, Germany, known for its verticality and multiple routes. The map rewards creative strategies and strong team coordination.

Vertigo

Vertigo is set atop a skyscraper under construction. Its unique vertical layout and risky drop-offs make for exciting and unpredictable matches.

Anubis

Anubis is an Egyptian-themed map with a mix of open spaces and tight corridors. Waterways and mid control are key to success on this map.

Office

Office is a classic hostage rescue map set in a snow-covered office building. It features tight hallways and lots of close-range action.

Train

Train is a classic map featuring a railway yard with multiple train cars and long sightlines. It requires sharp aim and good positioning to control the bombsites.

Cache

Cache is a fan-favorite map set in a Chernobyl-style industrial area. Its open mid and quick rotations make for dynamic gameplay.

Tuscan

Tuscan is a Mediterranean-themed map with a complex layout, rewarding creative strategies and teamwork. It was recently reintroduced to the competitive pool.

Italy

Italy is a classic hostage rescue map set in a beautiful Italian village. Its winding alleys and open courtyards create exciting rescue scenarios.

Aztec

Aztec is a classic map set in ancient ruins, featuring long-range combat and tricky water crossings. It is famous for its unique setting and weather effects.

Cobblestone

Cobblestone is a medieval-themed map with large open areas and tight corridors. Its unique layout encourages a variety of tactics.

Dust

Dust is the map that started it all, featuring a straightforward layout and quick gameplay. It paved the way for many future maps in the series.

Shortdust

Shortdust is a smaller, faster version of the classic Dust map, perfect for quick games and intense duels.

Shortnuke

Shortnuke offers a fast-paced, close-quarters experience based on the classic Nuke map, ideal for small teams and quick strategies.

Lake

Lake is a compact map set around a beautiful lakeside house, offering fast action and close-quarters combat.

Safehouse

Safehouse is a tiny map set in a countryside home, perfect for quick matches and close-range battles.

St. Marc

St. Marc is a bright, colorful map set in a coastal village, known for its destructible cover and lively setting.

Bank

Bank is a compact map set in a modern city bank, offering fast-paced action and tight corners.

Agency

Agency is a multi-level office map with elevators and plenty of vertical gameplay. It is a favorite for hostage rescue scenarios.

Assault

Assault is a legendary warehouse map, famous for its hostage rescue gameplay and tense firefights.

Canals

Canals is a visually stunning map set in a historic city, featuring water routes and open spaces for creative tactics.

CS2 Maps

Dust II

Dust II is one of the most iconic maps in Counter-Strike history, known for its balanced layout and intense mid control. The map features two bombsites and is set in a Middle Eastern town.

Mirage

Mirage is a popular competitive map set in a North African city. It offers a variety of strategic options for both teams, with open mid areas and well-defined bombsites.

Inferno

Inferno is famous for its narrow chokepoints and tactical gameplay. The map is set in an Italian village and requires strong team coordination, especially around the banana and apartments areas.

Nuke

Nuke is a unique map with a vertical layout, featuring both upper and lower bombsites. Set in a nuclear power plant, it challenges players with its complex rotations and multiple entry points.

Ancient

Ancient is a newer addition to the CS2 map pool, set in a mysterious jungle temple. It features tight corridors and open spaces, rewarding both aggressive and methodical playstyles.

Overpass

Overpass is a map set in Berlin, Germany, known for its verticality and multiple routes. The map rewards creative strategies and strong team coordination.

Vertigo

Vertigo is set atop a skyscraper under construction. Its unique vertical layout and risky drop-offs make for exciting and unpredictable matches.

Anubis

Anubis is an Egyptian-themed map with a mix of open spaces and tight corridors. Waterways and mid control are key to success on this map.

Train

Train is a classic map featuring a railway yard with multiple train cars and long sightlines. It requires sharp aim and good positioning to control the bombsites.

Cache

Cache is a fan-favorite map set in a Chernobyl-style industrial area. Its open mid and quick rotations make for dynamic gameplay.

Agency

Agency is a multi-level office map with elevators and plenty of vertical gameplay. It is a favorite for hostage rescue scenarios.
